About Me!

My favorite hobbies include outdoor sports such as snowboarding and hiking, learning new information in very random subjects, chess, exercising, and designing projects that further my understanding of software development. Computer science was my first choice of college major because of my fascination of computers. When I was 8 years old I discovered the technology culture of youtube, and from then on all I wanted was to experiment with computers. Once I tried to use an old Xbox as the case of a computer build when I was in the 8th grade for a school project. I completely broke the computer and the xbox, but the passion never died regardless of my failures with experimentation. This led me to eventually choose computer science as a major which I am grateful for because unlike most, I enjoy the topics I am being taught.
